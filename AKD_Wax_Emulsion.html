Features and Benefits

It is a versatile chemical that easily reacts with substances containing hydrogen atoms in their structural make-up to form an adduct that is highly repellent to water. AKD is an outstanding size for various forms of cellulosic materials especially paper.

As a kind of neutral sizing agent, the product has been widely used in paper-making industry because of its good consistent property. The product can be applied in a wide range of PH value, as a good alternative of traditional rosin.
