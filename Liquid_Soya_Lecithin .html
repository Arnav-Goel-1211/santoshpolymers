Product Description

A complex mixture of acetone-insoluble phosphatides is called Lecithin. It mainly consists of phosphatidylcholine (17.5% ), Phosphatidylethanolamine (15%) phosphatidylserine (1%) and phosphatidylinositol (10%). These are further combined with various other substances, like triglycerides, fatty acids, and carbohydrates that are separated from the crude vegetable oil.

Lecithins have emulsification and lubricant properties, and are a surfactant. In the paint industry, it forms protective coatings for surfaces with painting and printing ink, has antioxidant properties, helps as a rust inhibitor, is a colour-intensifying agent, catalyst, conditioning aid modifier, and dispersing aid; it is a good stabilizing and suspending agent, emulsifier, and wetting agent, helps in maintaining uniform mixture of several pigments, helps in grinding of metal oxide pigments, is a spreading and mixing aid, prevents hard settling of pigments, eliminates foam in water-based paints, and helps in fast dispersion of latex-based paints.
